— Common Questions

Things people
ask before they call.

If your question's not here, just ring. Daniel will pick up or call you back same day.

Q.01 How much does a concrete slab actually cost?

It depends on size, thickness, reinforcement, finish, and access — and anyone who quotes you sight-unseen is guessing. We come out, measure, and give you a written quote that breaks it down. Then you can compare apples to apples.

Q.02 How long does the job take?

A typical residential slab is 2–4 days on site: prep, reinforce, pour, finish. Then 5–7 days curing before you load it. We'll tell you upfront when you can park on it, walk on it, build on it.

Q.03 Do you do small jobs?

Yes. Side passages, garden bed footings, a single shed slab — if it makes sense for both of us, we'll do it. Same standard whether it's 4 square metres or 40.

Q.04 Are you licensed & insured?

Yes — fully insured, with public liability cover for residential and small commercial. Happy to send through the certificate of currency before we start.

Q.05 What if I'm not sure what I need?

That's literally what we're paid to figure out. Show us the space, tell us what you want it to do, and we'll tell you what makes sense — and what's a waste of money.
